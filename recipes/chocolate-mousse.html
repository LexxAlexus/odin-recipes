2 Minute Chocolate Mousse

Looking for an easy no-bakechocolate dessert recipe? It really doesn't get any easier than this heavenly homemade chocolate mousse!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 3 tsp powder sugar
  • 1 pinch sea salt (optional)

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients in a medium size bowl. The more you mix the thicker the mousse will get!
  2. Stiff until peak forms
  3. You are all done! You can pipe mousse in a fancy dish and add a few topping if you like.
