Yummy Chocolate Chip Cookies

These yummy chocolate-chip-cookies are perfect for any event. They are quick and easy to make with just a few steps you will have these cookies made in no time. Down below are the ingredients needed... lets get baking!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 3.5 oz box of yellow pudding mix
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven 350 degrees
  2. Combine flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. Stiff until it's all mixed together evenly
  3. Combine butter and sugars together in a mixing bowl
  4. Slowly adding in an egg after 30 seconds
  5. Stir in vanilla extract and pudding mix
  6. Add half of dry ingredients together
  7. Add in other half then pout chocolate chips in
  8. Roll dough into ball and place on baking sheet
  9. Let bake for 10-12 mins. Once done let cool for ten minutes
