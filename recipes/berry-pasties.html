Blueberry Pasties

These yummy blueberry pasties are perfect deserts for parties, birthdays, or even picnics. I forgot to mention they are the best when eating for breakfast. They are quick and simple to make and everyone will love them.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups blueberries
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  • 1 pack puff pasties
  • 1 egg
  • 8 oz cream cheese
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven 400 degrees
  2. In a small pot on medium heat bring blueberries and only half cup of sugar to a simmer. Add in lemon juice
  3. Cream together cream cheese, half cup sugar, and vanilla
  4. Cut puff pasties in half and line them on a baking sheet.
  5. Mix egg in bowl and spread egg along corners of pastries
  6. Add cream cheese in middle of each pastry then add blueberries on top
  7. Bake in oven for 15 minutes
  8. Let sit and cool for 10 minutes. Optional: you can add powder sugar on top
